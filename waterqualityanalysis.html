About App

This application is designed to Accurately Predict the Potability of the given sample of water with 98% accuracy with the help of Deep Learning Model (Artificial Neural Networks).

This application is intended for the people who want to check the potability of water at a very Low-cost or No-cost with the help of Deep Learning Model.
